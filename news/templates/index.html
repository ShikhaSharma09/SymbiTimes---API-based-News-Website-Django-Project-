About Us

“Symbi Times”, an online news website, aims to provide an aesthetic and appealing portal for the readers which provides up-to-date and reliable information about the current happenings around the globe in a summarized and simple words in an engaging user experience.

  • Inform: Provide timely, precise, and relevant news articles.
  • Entertain: Engage users with games, music, podcasts and quiz.
  • Connect: Foster community interaction through our chatbot and comment sections.

We want the user to be able to see and listen to what they want and not have any personal propaganda and agenda forced on them. We want to present a place that has all the current event going on around the world in one single place for the user’s convenience.

GAPS COVERED BY OUR WEBSITE

The problem that our website tackles are as follows:

  • 01 Majority biased for agenda or propaganda

    The current state of news channels and articles is abysmal, as a majority of them are biased pieces of content meant to push an agenda or further propaganda.

  • 02 Overstate minor issues as grand problems.

    The news articles currently are just over inflated talks about small or petty issues made to look like grand problems to occupy the users because of a lack of exciting news incoming.

  • 03 User-unfriendly websites with unnecessary advertisements.

    Most of the news website are not very user friendly and bombard the users with advertisements and suggestions that the user did not ask for.

Services

The “Symbi Times” aims to provide an extensive platform which caters to a wide range of interests. The purpose of the online news website is to give the public an unadulterated source of gathering information about current events around the world. We not only want to provide the users with news about the current happenings across the world, but to also give them a familiar place to spend their time.

Real Time

Make the news that appears in the newspaper update frequently in real time.

Radio Station

Adding a radio off to the side which acts like a regular radio where the user can select a station to listen to which updates in real time.

Chatbot

An AI-powered assistant that can answer queries and engage in friendly banter. The incorporation of a chatbot feature to provide assistance, answer queries, and engage them in informative conversations.

Small Games

Take a break from the news and challenge yourself with fun, interactive games. Add more interactive things like comics or games that one would find in a typical newspaper, like Sudoku, Crossword, Garfield, etc.
